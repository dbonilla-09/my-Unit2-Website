    • Brownie Recipe

    1. Pre-heat oven to 350F
    2. Get a 9x9 pan and cover with parchment paper or spray with baking oil
    3. Get 1 and 1/3 cup of sugar and put it into a food proccessor for 30-60 seconds or until the sugar turns into powder
    4. Melt 2/3 cups of butter
    5. Add your melted butter and grounded sugar to a bowl. Whisk by hand for around a mintue or until items become a smooth texture.
    6. Add 2 large eggs into your bowl and whisk again until eggs are fully combined with your mixture
    7. Sift 2/3 cup of flour, 1/2 cup of cocoa powder, 1/2 tsp of baking powder, and 3/4 tsp of salt directly into your bowl
    8. Mix all dry ingredients to your mixture. Mix with a folding motion using a rubber spatula
    9. Optional: Add and mix 100-150g of chopped chocolate or chocolate chips
    10. Spread your brownie batter into the pan
    11. Put your brownies in the oven and bake for 20-25 minutes.
    12. Once the brownies are done cooking, take them out and let them cool for another 20-25
    13. Once cooled they are ready for you to put on your Dubai spread

    Dubai Spread Recipe

    1. Cut up ahd shred 5 cups of kataifi
    2. Add 6 tbsp of butter, melted and cooke kataifi on a pan for 10 minutes or until brown
    3. Add kataifi and 1 and 1/3 cup of pistachio cream to a bowl and mix
    4. Optional: Add 3 tsbp of tahini and 1/2 tsp to 3/4 teaspoon of salt to pistachio mixture

    Dubai Chocolate Brownie Recipe

    1. Get your brownies and add kataifi and pistachio mixture ontop using a spatula
    2. Optional: Place brownies in the fridge for 10-20 mintues if you want kataifi mixture to harden
    3. In a small bowl add 1/2 cup of heavy cream and 150g of milk or semi-sweet chocolate
    4. Melt for a few seconds, stir, and keep putting it back in to melt and stir until chocolate is fully melted
    5. Pour the melted chocolate over your pistachio layer and spread until fully covered
